What this is

AuTomato Labs is a community robotics and maker lab for people who want to build, experiment, collaborate, and learn. We work on robotics, automation, hardware, software, fabrication, and other technical projects.

What happens here

  • Robotics and automation projects
  • Hands-on prototyping and experiments
  • Community build sessions
  • Technical collaboration across hardware and software

Who should join

Engineers, students, artists, tinkerers, and serious hobbyists are all welcome. You do not need to know everything. You do need to want to build.
